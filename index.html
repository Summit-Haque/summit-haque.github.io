About

I am a researcher and educator with experience in academic teaching, software engineering, usability studies, and generative AI research. I currently work at Oregon State University and also serve as an Assistant Professor at Shahjalal University of Science and Technology on study leave.

Current Research

FRONTIERS

NSF-funded collaborative research project

Improving assessment in software engineering education by developing and evaluating methods to assess the quality of individual contributions to team projects using software artifacts such as commits, issues, pull requests, and code reviews.

GenAI in Problem Solving

Comparative study

A study of undergraduate computing students in the United States and Bangladesh examining generative AI usage behaviors while solving programming problems and the impact on learning.
